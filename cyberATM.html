Cyber ATM

For my second programming course I decided to revive an old project I worked on. A very simpel, yet elegant ATM-machine with lots of cool features. My main goal for this refreshement was to add a fancy GUI to it and to get into Python. So I got two birds with one stone and managed to do them both. I used the built in GUI-framwork "Tkinter" to display different labels and buttons.
